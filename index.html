Prototype1

Visualization 1

Interpretation

This visualization shows the number of incidents in each day of Dec/2018(the xAxis shows the each day of of month and the yAxis shows the number of incidents, and the grey line is the average line.) and as we could predicted, the number of incidents increases at weekends (mostly fridays) also the least number of incidents happend at Christmas day.

Discussion

The primary goal of this visualization was finding the number of incidents in each day of Dec/2018 and finding the realation between each day (holidays and normal days) and the number of incidents. Since it was my first time drawing Bar Charts!

Credit

The source of data is datasf.org.

Prototype2

Visualization 2

Interpretation

This visualization shows the number of police incidents in city of San Franciscio during Dec/2018 based on 24hrs time intervals. This visualization is dynamic and by mouseover will show the number of incidents at that time interval.

Discussion

My goal was to observe how the number of incidents change during day and night time, althogh the number of incidents depends on different factors, this visualization gives us a good insight about the major factors involving with the rise and fall of the insident number.

Credit

The source of data is datasf.org.

Prototype3

Visualization 3

Interpretation

This is a pie chart with a legend, shows the top 6 criminal police incidents in the city of San Franciscio during Dec/2018. This visualization is dynamic and with mouseover will show the name and percentage of the criminal activity.

Discussion

I was curious to find the major criminal activies in city of San Franciscio. Burglary, larency theft and robbery is by far the most criminal incident in the city of San Franciscio.

Credit

The source of data is datasf.org.
